PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT
This Professional Services Agreement is entered into between Meridian Consulting Group LLC (the "Supplier") and the customer identified in the Statement of Work (the "Customer"), under which the Supplier will provide the consulting services and deliverables described in each Statement of Work.
1. Services
The Supplier shall provide the services described in each Statement of Work on a time-and-materials basis. Any timelines, estimates or staffing levels are indicative only and do not bind the Supplier.
2. Acceptance
Each deliverable shall be deemed accepted unless the Customer gives written notice of rejection, specifying the reasons for rejection, within fifteen (15) days of delivery. The Customer shall pay for all deliverables that are accepted, and the Supplier shall correct any non-conforming deliverable at its own cost within a reasonable time.
3. Fees and Expenses
The Customer shall pay all fees on a time-and-materials basis in accordance with the rates set out in each Statement of Work, together with all pre-approved expenses at cost, within thirty (30) days of the invoice date. The Supplier may not revise its rates during the term of any Statement of Work. All fees are non-refundable.
4. Intellectual Property
All intellectual property rights in any deliverables and work product specifically created for the Customer under this Agreement shall vest exclusively in the Customer. The Supplier retains ownership of its pre-existing methodologies and materials. The Customer is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use the Supplier's pre-existing methodologies and materials incorporated in the Deliverables for its internal business purposes.
5. Warranties
The services and deliverables are provided with the warranties set out in this Section 5. The Supplier warrants that (a) the services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner by qualified personnel, (b) each deliverable will conform to the specifications set out in the applicable Statement of Work, and (c) the deliverables will be free from material defects. The Supplier's sole obligation for breach of the foregoing warranties shall be to re-perform the services or correct the deliverable at no additional cost to the Customer. All other warranties are disclaimed to the fullest extent permitted by law.
6. Personnel
The Supplier shall not substitute, reassign or remove any personnel designated as key personnel in a Statement of Work without the Customer's prior written consent, which consent shall not be unreasonably withheld. For other personnel, the Supplier shall use reasonable efforts to maintain continuity and shall provide qualified replacements.
7. Indemnity
Each party shall indemnify, defend and hold harmless the other party and its personnel against any and all claims, losses, damages, liabilities and expenses arising from or in connection with the indemnifying party's breach of this Agreement. The Supplier shall further indemnify, defend and hold harmless the Customer against any claim that the deliverables or the Supplier's services infringe any third party's intellectual property rights.
8. Limitation of Liability
Subject to the exceptions below, each party's aggregate liability arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by the Customer in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. In no event shall either party be liable for any indirect, incidental or consequential damages, or for any loss of profits, revenue or data. The limitations of liability in this Section 8 shall not apply to (i) a party's breach of confidentiality obligations, (ii) a party's indemnification obligations under Section 7, (iii) infringement of the other party's intellectual property rights, or (iv) gross negligence or wilful misconduct.
9. Term and Termination
This Agreement continues until each Statement of Work is completed. Either party may terminate this Agreement or any Statement of Work for convenience at any time on thirty (30) days notice; either party may terminate. On termination by either party, the Customer shall pay for all work performed up to the effective date of termination at the rates set out in the applicable Statement of Work, and shall not be liable for any further fees under the terminated Statement of Work.
10. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Illinois, and the parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Illinois.
