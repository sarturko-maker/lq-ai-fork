MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT
This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement is entered into between Aegis Analytics Inc. (the "Discloser") and the counterparty identified in the signature block (the "Recipient"), to protect Confidential Information exchanged in connection with a potential business relationship.
1. Definitions
"Confidential Information" means all information disclosed by either party to the other, in any form and whether or not marked confidential, including all business, technical, financial and commercial information and any notes or analyses derived from it. All Confidential Information remains the property of the disclosing party.
2. Confidentiality Obligations
Each party shall hold in strict confidence all Confidential Information of the other party and shall not disclose it to any third party or use it for any purpose other than evaluating the potential business relationship, without the prior written consent of the disclosing party.
3. Exclusions
Information is not Confidential Information to the extent that: (a) it is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement by the receiving party; (b) it was already in the receiving party's possession without obligation of confidentiality at the time of disclosure; (c) it is independently developed by the receiving party without use of or reference to the Confidential Information; or (d) the receiving party is required to disclose it by law, regulation or court order, provided the receiving party gives the disclosing party prompt notice and reasonable cooperation to limit the disclosure.
4. Term
Each party's obligations of confidentiality under this Agreement shall survive for a period of five (5) years from the date of disclosure after the return or destruction of the Confidential Information.
5. Return of Information
Upon the disclosing party's written demand, the receiving party shall immediately return or destroy all Confidential Information of the disclosing party and all copies, and shall certify such destruction in writing. The receiving party may not retain any copy for any reason, except that the receiving party may retain copies to the extent required by applicable law or regulation, or as part of routine backup or archival processes, provided such copies remain subject to the confidentiality obligations of this Agreement.
6. No Licence
Nothing in this Agreement grants either party any licence or right in the other party's Confidential Information, and either party may pursue any opportunity with any third party without obligation to the other.
7. Injunctive Relief
Each party acknowledges that any breach by the other of its confidentiality obligations would cause irreparable harm and agrees that the non-breaching party shall be entitled to injunctive relief without the requirement to post a bond or prove actual damages, in addition to any other remedy.
8. No Warranty
All Confidential Information is provided "AS IS". Neither party makes any representation or warranty as to the accuracy or completeness of its Confidential Information and neither party shall have any liability to the other arising from the other's use of or reliance on it.
9. Indemnity
Each party shall indemnify, defend and hold harmless the other party and its affiliates against any and all claims, losses, damages, liabilities and expenses to the extent arising from or in connection with the indemnifying party's breach of its confidentiality obligations under this Agreement.
10. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California, and the parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of California.
